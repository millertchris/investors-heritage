  • Full Benefit

    A HEALTHY OPTION.

    Our Full Benefit option is best for clients who are in good health, do not want to pay one lump sum, and don't mind answering a few health questions

  • What it does.

    Our Full Benefit product allows clients who satisfactorily answer three health questions to qualify for 1st-day full benefit coverage. Installment payments may be made monthly, quarterly, semi-annually, or annually.

  • How To Qualify.

    To qualify, the applicant must have acceptable answers to alI health questions. A Medical Information Bureau check will be conducted to verify the answers provided. If the applicant does not qualify for Full Benefit, a Graded Benefit plan will be issued automatically.
